Android System Permissions

The Service requires the INTERNET permission in order to obtain arrival times from the Open Data iniciative of Zaragoza's city council and to update stops and lines information.

The Service requires the CAMERA permission in order to scan QR codes.

The Service requires the LOCATION permission in order to display the user location in the map and to display nearby stops.
